We are a bunch of nature enthusiast student from NIFT Chennai. We love planting and delivering awesome home decors through out plants. We have over 4000+ unbiased reviews and our curstomers trust our plant process and delivery service every time.

Our vision is to promote healthy habbit of planting more and more trees around our environment. We want to reduce the usage of plastic and wood products which are used to decoraate our interiors. We use small and trendy clay pots to grow out plants.
